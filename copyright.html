WH2 Copyright & DMCA Policy

Effective Date: 2026

WH2 Radio respects intellectual property rights and expects all users, artists, contributors, and content providers to do the same.

Copyright Ownership

All music, artwork, logos, videos, graphics, images, lyrics, recordings, and creative works remain the property of their respective owners.

Authorized Content Only

Users may only upload, submit, stream, distribute, or share content they own or have written permission to use.

DMCA Takedown Requests

If you believe copyrighted material has been used without permission, please submit a written notice containing:

  • Your contact information
  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • Location of the content in question
  • A statement that you believe the use is unauthorized
  • Your signature or authorized representation

Content Removal

WH2 Radio reserves the right to remove content immediately while investigating copyright concerns.

Repeat Violations

Users who repeatedly submit unauthorized content may lose access to WH2 Radio services.

Counter Notices

If content was removed in error, a counter-notice may be submitted for review.
