Before You Submit

WH2 Radio accepts AI-generated music only.

  • Submit AI-created songs, AI-assisted tracks, or original AI music concepts only.
  • Do not submit Apple Music, Spotify, YouTube Music, or phone file links.
  • Do not submit copyrighted commercial songs.
  • Do not submit unauthorized artist voices, cloned celebrity voices, stolen beats, copyrighted samples, or music you do not have permission to use.
  • Audio link must be public and accessible.

Accepted audio formats: MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, OGG.

Accepted cover formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, SVG.
