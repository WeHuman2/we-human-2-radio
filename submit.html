Submit Music to WH2 Radio

WH2 Radio accepts AI-assisted music, AI-generated music, and independent human-created music for review. Submission does not guarantee approval, airplay, playlist placement, promotion, or exposure.

Before You Submit

  • Submit original music or music you have legal permission to submit.
  • AI-assisted and AI-generated music is welcome.
  • Independent human-created music is welcome.
  • Do not submit copyrighted commercial songs you do not own.
  • Do not submit unauthorized cloned voices, stolen beats, or unlicensed samples.
  • For now, provide public review links. Direct uploads will be added in a later phase.
