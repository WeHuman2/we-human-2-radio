Submit Human Music to WH2 Radio

Independent artists can submit original music for WH2 Radio review. Submission does not guarantee approval, airplay, playlist placement, promotion, or exposure.

Before You Submit

  • Submit original music only.
  • Do not submit copyrighted commercial songs you do not own.
  • Do not submit unauthorized samples, stolen beats, or music you do not have permission to use.
  • Do not submit Apple Music, Spotify, YouTube Music, or phone file links.
  • Audio and cover links must be public and accessible.

Accepted audio formats: MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, OGG.

Accepted cover formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, SVG.
