The public player is what listeners see.

  • Listen to music
  • View Featured Artist

  • View legal pages

Open Player

👑 Owner Dashboard

The dashboard is your control center.

  • Turn radio ON
  • Turn radio OFF
  • Update song information
  • Update cover art
  • Update Featured Artist
  • Review submissions
  • Manage library
  • View analytics
  • Share radio link

Open Dashboard

⭐ Featured Artist

Use the Featured Artist section in the dashboard to update:

  • Artist Name
  • Artist Bio
  • Artist Image
  • Artist Website / Social Link

The featured artist appears at the top of the WH2 Radio player.

🎤 Music Submissions

Artists can submit:

  • Artist Name
  • Song Title
  • Email Address
  • Music Link
  • Cover Art Link
  • Notes

Open Submission Form

📥 Review Submissions

Inside the dashboard:

  • View artist submissions
  • Open music links
  • Open cover links
  • Approve artists
  • Reject artists

Approved submissions can be added to the library.

🎵 Approved Songs Library

The Approved Songs Library stores approved music.

  • Add songs
  • Delete songs
  • View songs
  • Make songs live

Open Library

🔄 Auto Playlist

WH2 Radio now supports playlist rotation.

When multiple approved songs exist in the library, the player can automatically move to the next song when the current song ends.

📊 Analytics

  • Rejected Submissions
  • Total Songs In Library
